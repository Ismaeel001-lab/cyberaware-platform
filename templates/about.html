About CyberAware

Project Title

Design and Implementation of an Interactive Cybersecurity Awareness and Assessment Platform for University Students

Developed By

Ismaila shehu wali
Admission No: NWUS/FCS/CYS/22/046
Department of Cybersecurity
North-West University Sokoto (NWUS)

Motivation

University students face growing cybersecurity threats phishing, social engineering, scams, and privacy risks many of which target Nigerian students specifically through platforms like WhatsApp and social media. Yet formal cybersecurity awareness education remains limited on most campuses. This platform was built to close that gap through practical, engaging, and locally relevant learning.

Key Objectives

1. Assess the current level of cybersecurity awareness among NWUS students through pre-test evaluation.
2. Deliver an interactive learning experience through gamified quizzes, real-world scenario simulations, and educational content on common cyber threats.
3. Evaluate the platform's effectiveness in improving cybersecurity awareness through pre- and post-test comparison.

This platform was developed as a final-year project for the Department of Cybersecurity, North-West University Sokoto, with guidance from cybersecurity professionals to ensure the content reflects real, current threats.
